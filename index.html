How to Play Cricket Trump Cards

üéØ Game Objective

Win all the cards by choosing the best stats to battle against the computer!

üéÆ How to Play

  • Each player starts with 20 random cards from 211 cricket legends
  • TURN-BASED SYSTEM: Winner of previous round chooses stat for next round
  • Player's Turn: You choose a stat, both cards compared using that same stat
  • Computer's Turn: Computer chooses a stat, both cards compared using that same stat
  • First round: You always choose first
  • Higher value wins the round (except for bowling stats where lower is better)
  • Winner takes both cards, loser loses their card
  • In case of a tie, cards go to bottom of deck
  • Game ends when one player runs out of cards

üìä Stat Guide

  • Higher is Better: Runs, Batting Average, Strike Rate, Wickets, Catches
  • Lower is Better: Bowling Average, Economy Rate
  • Matches: More experience is better
  • Centuries & Fifties: More is better
